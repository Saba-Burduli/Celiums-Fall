# Game Design

Aren betrayed Lord Celium's occult faction and uses its corrupted magic to protect common people. The intended feel is fast, readable top-down combat in oppressive but legible spaces.

The run has three areas. Forest enemies teach movement and resource use. The shrine locks the exit until the Mire Priest dies. Black Mountain culminates in Lord Celium, whose second phase becomes faster and adds summons. Four stones permanently improve health, mana, magic, and movement during the run. The Old Villager's moonstone quest rewards max health.

Combat should reward spacing: melee is free and close, magic is ranged but costs mana, dash avoids danger. Death resets the run. Primitive silhouettes and distinct colors communicate enemy roles without assets.
